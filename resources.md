# Dutch Learning Resources

A curated collection of resources for learning Dutch at every level.

## Dictionaries & Translation

- [Van Dale Online](https://www.vandale.nl/) — The definitive Dutch dictionary
- [Linguee](https://www.linguee.com/english-dutch) — Contextual translations with real-world examples
- [WordReference](https://www.wordreference.com/ennl/) — Community-powered dictionary with forums
- [Google Translate](https://translate.google.com/) — Quick translations (always double-check!)
- [Reverso Context](https://context.reverso.net/translation/dutch-english/) — Translations in context

## Pronunciation

- [Forvo](https://forvo.com/languages/nl/) — Hear native speakers pronounce any Dutch word
- [Heardutchhere.net](https://www.heardutchhere.net/) — Dutch pronunciation guide with audio
- Search YouTube for "Dutch pronunciation guide" for video tutorials

## Online Courses & Apps

- [Duolingo Dutch](https://www.duolingo.com/course/nl/en/Learn-Dutch) — Free, gamified course
- [DutchPod101](https://www.dutchpod101.com/) — Audio/video lessons at multiple levels
- [Babbel Dutch](https://www.babbel.com/learn-dutch) — Structured course with speech recognition
- [Memrise](https://www.memrise.com/) — Vocabulary building with spaced repetition
- [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net/) — Flashcard app (use with vocabulary.json from this project!)

## Grammar Reference

- [dutchgrammar.com](https://www.dutchgrammar.com/) — Comprehensive grammar explanations
- [Taalunie](https://taalunie.org/) — Official Dutch Language Union
- [zichtbaarnederlands.nl](https://www.zichtbaarnederlands.nl/) — Visual Dutch grammar

## Dutch Media

- [NOS Journaal](https://nos.nl/) — Dutch national news (great for listening practice)
- [NPO Start](https://npo.nl/) — Dutch public TV streaming
- [NPO Radio 1](https://www.nporadio1.nl/) — Dutch talk radio
- Search Spotify for "Dutch music" or "Nederlandse muziek" playlists
- [Jort Kelder](https://www.youtube.com/) — Search for Dutch YouTube channels for immersion

## Reading Practice

- [NOS Jeugdjournaal](https://jeugdjournaal.nl/) — News in simpler Dutch (aimed at children, great for learners)
- [Het Parool](https://www.parool.nl/) — Amsterdam newspaper
- [De Volkskrant](https://www.volkskrant.nl/) — Quality Dutch newspaper
- Dutch Wikipedia — Try reading articles on topics you already know

## Speaking Practice & Community

- [iTalki](https://www.italki.com/) — Find Dutch tutors for 1-on-1 lessons
- [Tandem](https://www.tandem.net/) — Language exchange app
- [r/learndutch](https://www.reddit.com/r/learndutch/) — Active Reddit community for Dutch learners
- [r/Dutch](https://www.reddit.com/r/Dutch/) — General Dutch language subreddit
- Search Facebook for "Learn Dutch" groups

## Exam Preparation

- [Inburgering exam](https://www.inburgeren.nl/) — If you need to pass the Dutch civic integration exam
- [NT2 exam](https://www.nt2.nl/) — Dutch as a second language certification

## Tips for Self-Study

- Set a daily goal (even 15 minutes helps)
- Label objects in your house with Dutch sticky notes
- Change your phone language to Dutch
- Listen to Dutch podcasts during your commute
- Keep a vocabulary notebook and review regularly
- Don't be afraid to make mistakes — Dutch people appreciate the effort!
