The A2 level builds on A1 foundations. At this stage you can handle everyday situations with more nuance: describing feelings, talking about your home, ordering food with specific preferences, discussing work and education, and connecting ideas with richer grammar. This list contains approximately 550 words and phrases that are incremental to the A1 list, aligned with CEFR/NT2 standards. Master these and you will be well-prepared for the NT2 Staatsexamen Programma I.

> **Tip**: Many A2 words are separable verbs (*scheidbare werkwoorden*) like *opbellen*, *uitdoen*, and *aantrekken*. In main clauses the prefix separates: *Ik trek mijn jas aan.* Learning the particle patterns now will pay off enormously at B1.
