The A1 level covers basic survival vocabulary: greetings, numbers, everyday objects, simple verbs, and the phrases you need to get through your first conversations in Dutch. This list is aligned with the CEFR/NT2 framework and contains roughly 300 of the most essential words and expressions for absolute beginners. Master these and you will be able to introduce yourself, shop, order food, ask for directions, and handle simple daily interactions.
